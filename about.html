Donate Hope is a non-profit organization founded in March 2023 by the Tech Elites , a group under the Women Techsters Cohort 2.5 and sponsored by Techdev.

Donate Hope was created with the main purpose of eradicating poverty globally by providing basic amenities to communities in need through partnering with charitable individuals and organizations.

We believe that overcoming poverty is the protection of fundamental human right, the right to dignity and a decent life.
Our mission is to ensure the eradication of poverty by at least 50% by 2030, we aim to work around the globe and provide the basic needs to those who cannot provide for themselves and help them become self-reliant.Our projects are focused on the basic needs of children like education and health.
Concurrently our projects always aim at sustainability by organizing other money generating initiatives such as agricultural projects.The goal is that these communities can continue to provide for their own population and strive for a world where children grow up healthy.

Due to the high number of families being affected by economical changes as well as climate changes we have come up with Poverty eradication project a team.Our goal is to meet the basic needs of those who are unable to do so on their own (or who are not otherwise supported) and to assist them in developing self-sufficiency and a sense of dignity.The Stop Poverty initiatives are centered on meeting people's fundamental needs, including health and education.
