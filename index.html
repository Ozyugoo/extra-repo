Poverty Eradication is a non-profit organization founded in March 2023 by the Tech Elites, a group under the Women Techsters Cohort 2.5 and sponsored by Techdev.

Poverty Eradication was created with the main purpose of eradicating poverty globally by providing basic amenities to communities in need through partnering with charitable individuals and organizations.

We belive that overcoming poverty is the protection of fundamental human right, the right to dignity and a decent life.
Our mission is to ensure the eradication of poverty by at least 50% by 2030. We aim to work around the globe and provide the basic needs to those who cannot provide for themselves and help them become self-reliant. Our projects are focused on the basic needs of the vulnerable such as education and health.
Concurrently our projects always aim at sustainability by organizing other money generating initiatives such as auction sales and other projects. The goal is that these communities can continue to provide for their own population and strive for a world where everyone is healthy.
Due to the high number of families being affected by economical changes as well as climate changes, we have come up with Poverty Eradication project. Our goal is to meet the basic needs of those who are unable to do so on their own (or who are not otherwise supported) and to assist them in developing self-sufficiency and a sense of dignity. The Poverty Eradication initiatives are centered on meeting people's fundamental needs, including health and education.

What We Do?

Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much.

Healthy Food

Healthy food is essential for human survival, healthy food improves one's mental and physical ability. By choosing a variety of healthy foods and practicing healthy eating habits, we can improve our overall health and well-being. We desire to help provide sufficient foods for communities.

Water & Sanitation

Lack of access to clean water and sanitation disproportionately affects the world's poorest and most vulnerable populations, including women and children. We are working to curb the inadequate access to clean water and proper sanitation which can lead to the spread of waterborne diseases, causing illness and even death.

Health Care

Access to health care is essential. One of the most significant barriers to healthcare access is the cost of healthcare, not everyone can afford to pay for medical services. We are committed to help communities build health clinic, train health care workers and invest in equpiment and medicine so everyone can grow up healthy and strong.

Increase Access to Education

Education is a key factor in reducing poverty. Providing access to education for all, regardless of social status, gender or race, can lead to better job opportunities and a higher income.

Improve Health Care

Healthcare is crucial for people living in poverty. Access to basic healthcare can help prevent diseases and illnesses, which can lead to more productive lives.

Economic growth is another essential factor in reducing poverty. Governments and organizations can invest in infrastructure, create jobs, and support small businesses to stimulate economic growth.
